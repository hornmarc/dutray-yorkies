Life Is Better With a Yorkie

From time to time, we have adult Yorkies available for adoption. These are retired breeding dogs or adults looking for their forever home. All are healthy, well-socialized, and AKC registered.

Currently Available Adults

There are no adults available at this time. Please contact Tracy or join the waiting list to be notified when an adult becomes available.

Why Adopt an Adult Yorkie?

  • Already house-trained and well-mannered
  • Known temperament and personality
  • Past the demanding puppy stage
  • Calm, loving companions ready for a quiet home
  • All veterinary records and AKC registration included

If you are interested in adopting a retired adult Yorkie, please get in touch. We carefully match our dogs with the right families.
